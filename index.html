About

ST117

An artist driven by a deep passion for visual expression — working across painting, drawing, photography and printmaking. Every piece carries the weight of history and the pulse of the present.

Rooted in Albanian culture and history, the work draws from the mountains, the struggle, the eagle — transforming personal and national identity into images that endure.

The Practice

Working across disciplines — each medium informs the others. A practice built on obsession, patience and the belief that art leaves marks history cannot erase.

Albanian Roots

Inspired by the resilience of Albanian history — Skanderbeg, the eagle of Illyria, the mountains of Shqipëria. Heritage is not nostalgia. It is fuel.

03
The Print

Printmaking as resistance. Every plate, every pull, every layer is deliberate and permanent. The print does not apologize for its mark.
